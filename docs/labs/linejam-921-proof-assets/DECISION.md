# Linejam 921 Proof Assets Lab Decision

## Options

1. Artifact plus reveal reel: full poem text beside CSS-only reveal motion and a public recap link.
2. Ticket stub: proof styled as a game-night admission ticket.
3. Round ledger: poem lines paired with the 1-2-3-4-5-4-3-2-1 structure.
4. Poetry poster: oversized finished poem as a standalone poster.
5. Before and after: one visible line contrasted with the complete reveal.
6. Proof stack: three compact modules for poem, reveal, and recap.

## Lock-In

Chosen: option 1, artifact plus reveal reel.

Why: the acceptance criteria are about proof, not just visual polish. Option 1
keeps the finished poem readable as real HTML text, places a self-contained
CSS reveal-in-motion asset next to it, and gives the public Room BFIO recap a
clear route. The other options either demote the poem, over-explain the rules,
or make the recap feel secondary.

Source artifact: public Room BFIO recap at `https://www.linejam.app/recap/BFIO`.

Locked copy:

- "A finished poem from Room BFIO"
- "Watch the reveal build line by line."
- "Open the public recap"

Taste check: concrete claims only; no anthropomorphized "refuses to X" style
tagline; existing Lucide scroll-text mark remains the only logo mark.
