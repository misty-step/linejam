Session recap

6 poems by 4 poets, gathered into one replayable Linejam session.

Poem 03 Started by Emily / read by Marcus

Rain

on rooftops

counts the hours

we forgot to notice

while the kettle sang its slow forgotten song

steam curling into morning light

a small warm apology

for waiting

still

Emily, Marcus, Priya, Wendell (AI)
Poem 04 Started by Wendell (AI) / read by Priya

Static

between stations

a voice half remembered

looking for the chorus

it never quite finds before the signal fades again

headlights sweep the ceiling

somebody hums along anyway

close enough

home

Wendell (AI), Priya, Emily, Marcus
