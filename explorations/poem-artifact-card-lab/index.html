Eight structurally distinct treatments of the same nine-line poem (word-shape 1,2,3,4,5,4,3,2,1), each rendered in the live theme-registry palette. Switch themes to check every option holds up across all four — the export renderer must work in whichever theme is active, not just Kenya. Sample poem is fictional; author names are placeholders, one marked AI to verify the attribution requirement (criterion 3) reads cleanly in every layout.

01 Stamp Ledger
Closest to the existing opengraph-image.tsx pattern — left-aligned stack, thin primary-color rule, circular stamp mark, sans-serif metadata line.

Rain

on rooftops

counts the hours

we forgot to notice

while the kettle sang its slow forgotten song

steam curling into morning light

a small warm apology

for waiting

still
